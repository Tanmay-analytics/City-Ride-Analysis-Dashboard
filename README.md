# **🚖 City Ride Analysis Dashboard**

## **📖 Project Overview**

This project presents a detailed analysis of city-wide ride-booking operations, aimed at uncovering patterns in urban mobility. By processing raw booking data, this dashboard provides stakeholders with actionable insights into ride success rates, cancellation reasons, revenue trends, and service quality ratings for both drivers and customers.

## **🎯 Project Objective**

The primary objective of this project is to analyze the ride-booking lifecycle to optimize service fulfillment and driver-customer satisfaction. Based on the dashboard metrics, the analysis aims to:

* **Operational Health Check:** Track and visualize the breakdown of **Booking Status** (Completed, Cancelled by Customer, Cancelled by Driver, No Driver Found) to identify fulfillment gaps.  
* **Revenue & Value Analysis:** Monitor **Total Booking Value** and revenue distribution across different **Payment Methods** (UPI, Cash, Credit/Debit Cards).  
* **Churn & Friction Mapping:** Investigate the top **Cancellation Reasons** for both drivers and customers to mitigate booking drops.  
* **Quality Benchmarking:** Measure and segment **Driver and Customer Ratings** by vehicle type (Auto, eBike, Go Mini, Go Sedan) to maintain high service standards.

## **📈 Optimization & Growth Opportunities**

By leveraging the insights from this dashboard, the following strategic actions can be taken to significantly enhance overall performance:

* **Reduce 'No Driver Found' Occurrences:** Identifying specific times and locations with high "No Driver Found" statuses allows for targeted driver incentives, ensuring better supply-demand matching and capturing lost revenue.  
* **Minimize Cancellations:** By addressing the top reasons for cancellations (such as "Personal/Car issues" for drivers or "Change of plans" for customers), we can implement features like better scheduling or driver support to increase the **Ride Success Rate**.  
* **Targeted Fleet Expansion:** Analyzing demand by **Vehicle Type** reveals which segments (e.g., eBikes or Go Mini) are underserved, guiding future fleet investments.  
* **Enhanced Customer Retention:** Utilizing ratings to identify low-performing segments allows for proactive quality control and training, leading to higher customer lifetime value.

## **🛠️ Tools & Technologies Used**

* **Power BI Desktop:** For creating interactive visualizations and the final analytical report.  
* **Power Query:** For cleaning the rideBookings.csv dataset, handling null values, and formatting dates/times.  
* **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions):** To calculate complex measures such as:  
  * *Ride Success Rate (%)*  
  * *Average Driver/Customer Ratings*  
  * *Total Revenue by Category*  
* **CSV Data:** The source dataset containing over 100,000 ride records including location, distance, and booking status.

## **📊 Key Insights**

* **Booking Success:** A significant portion of bookings are successful, but "No Driver Found" remains a key bottleneck during peak hours.  
* **Cancellation Trends:** Customers frequently cancel due to "Change of plans," while drivers often cite "Personal & Car related issues."  
* **Preferred Payments:** Digital payments (UPI and Credit Cards) show a higher transaction volume compared to traditional cash.  
* **Service Excellence:** Both Driver and Customer ratings average around **4.0/5.0**, indicating a generally healthy ecosystem with room for optimization in specific vehicle segments like *Go Mini*.

## **🚀 How to Use**

1. Download the **City Ride Analysis dashboard.pbix** file.  
2. Open the file using **Power BI Desktop**.  
3. If the data doesn't load automatically, ensure **rideBookings.csv** is in the same directory or update the data source settings.  
4. Use the interactive slicers on the dashboard to filter by **Date**, **Vehicle Type**, and **Booking Status**.

## **🏁 Conclusion**

The City Ride Analysis Dashboard provides a data-driven framework for improving urban transport services. By addressing the high volume of "No Driver Found" status and understanding cancellation triggers, the service can improve its fulfillment rate and overall customer satisfaction. The insights regarding payment preferences also suggest a strong lean toward a cashless ecosystem.
